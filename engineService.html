Engine Repair services


Why It’s Important

Because you count on your vehicle for safe, reliable transportation, it’s important to keep it in good condition through proper maintenance and timely repairs. Regardless of the make or model you drive, the ASE-certified technicians at SAAR Auto Repair Workshop, are here to help you with all your engine repair needs. Zepeda Auto Service is your one-stop complete auto shop, so schedule your next appointment for engine repair using our online scheduler or by calling us.

Prevention And Common Repairs

One of the more important aspects of engine repair is the preventive maintenance services performed at our shop. SAAR Auto Services provides routine oil/filter changes, fluid checks/tops/changes/flushes, and more. Additionally, we take care of planned periodic work, such as tune-ups. Finally, when you encounter a major and/or unexpected problem with your vehicle, our team takes care of a comprehensive range of engine repairs. For instance, our professional technicians accurately diagnose and repair the underlying causes of an illuminated check engine light. Also, if your automobile is overheating, we can fix the cooling system–whether the trouble lies with the radiator, fan, hoses, water pump, or thermostat. Furthermore, we repair all problems associated with combustion and powering your transportation. Regarding the fuel system, look to us for assistance with clogged fuel injectors, failing fuel pumps, or other components. We also handle the timing belts/chains that keep your engine running smoothly. What’s more, we’re skilled at exhaust system work. Specific repairs your car might need include catalytic converter replacement as well as repair/replacement of the exhaust manifold, tailpipe(s), muffler, exhaust pipe, oxygen sensor, and resonator. If starting your auto becomes an issue, see us for help with the battery, starter, or alternator. Since the engine’s power is only as good as your vehicle’s ability to transfer it to the wheels, let us make any necessary transmission repairs, too.
