Common Signs You Need Brake Repair

We all hate surprises. But when unexpected problems put your safety at risk, you must deal with them immediately. If your vehicle has brake problems, you might notice some early warning signs. Some of the most common symptoms that your car needs brake repair includes high-pitched screeching noises when you brake, visible wear and tear of the brake pads, slow stopping response, and a vibrating brake pedal or steering wheel when you brake. More signs are loud grinding sounds from your brake pads, puddles of fluid underneath your parked car, or if your vehicle is pulling to one side when you brake. Think you have a brake problem?
