AUTO A/C REPAIR IN SAAR


WE’LL RESTORE YOUR DRIVING COMFORT

Keep Your Cool

When it comes to an optimal driving experience, your vehicle shouldn’t just be safe. It should also be comfortable. An efficiently working A/C system is a must-have during hot summer days. If your car’s HVAC system isn’t functioning correctly, the cabin can become a sweltering—and even dangerous—environment. Fortunately, help is just a phone call away. The ASE-certified car A/C repair mechanics at Zepeda Auto Service have the tools and training to help Chicago motorists beat the heat. We can repair and maintain cooling systems in all makes and models, including domestic and import vehicles. We also back our car A/C repairs with TechNet 24/24 and NAPA 24/24 warranties. This ensures you will keep your cool wherever you go. Have questions? Our staff will gladly address any concerns you might have. If your car’s A/C system is blowing warm air, smells muggy, or makes strange noises, call our auto repair shop today at 872-274-2721 to book an appointment. We are open Saturday through thursday, sat-thu: 8am – 6pm, and Closed Friday.

What Auto A/C Repairs Do We Do?

Some of the most common types of car A/C repairs we do here at SAAR Auto Service include recharging your vehicle’s A/C, repairing the A/C compressor and condenser, fixing the A/C evaporator, changing the refrigerant fluid, and replacing the cabin air filter. These services ensure your car’s air conditioning system will work at its best, keeping you cool and comfortable. We offer quality A/C repair services for all makes and models. To learn more or to book your appointment, call us today at 0348-9220242. Our auto repair shop is conveniently located at Near General Bus Stand Timergara. We’ll keep your car’s A/C system in optimal condition, elevating your driving comfort.
